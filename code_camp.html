About Summer Camp

Summer Camp is a two weeks training that is focused on building ICT capacity for young People The Training was divided to two major categorie, Mobile App Development and Web Development using, html, css, php, mysql(for web) and MIT App Invetor for mobile development. At the end of the programme, Students succesfully built websites that solved a problem and mobile app that provides solutions. The Programme was educative and fun for the students.

Summer Camp was Prodly sponsored by: UNESCO and The Zaria Elizabeth Foundation
